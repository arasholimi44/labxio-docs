Welcome to *Labxio Free*! We are deeply committed to safeguarding your privacy. Important Medical Disclaimer

Labxio Free is designed strictly for *informational purposes* and is not a medical device. The app provides analysis of lab results and general health insights based on structured interpretation logic derived from standard clinical guidelines.

Labxio Free *does not offer medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment*. It should never be used as a substitute for professional medical consultation, diagnosis, or decision-making. *Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare provider* with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or treatment plan.

Never delay, ignore, or disregard professional medical advice based on any information provided by this app. *Your well-being is paramount, and Labxio Free is not a tool for self-diagnosis or self-treatment.*

1. What We Don't Collect

Labxio Free is built with a fundamental principle: *your privacy comes first*. This means we are designed to operate without needing to collect your personal data. We want to be unequivocally clear:

  • We *do not* collect, store, or transmit any *personal information* such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or any other identifying details.
  • We *do not* collect or store any of your sensitive *lab results or health data*. This information remains solely on your device.
  • We *do not* track or collect any *device identifiers* that could be linked back to you.
  • We *do not* collect any *location data*.
  • We *do not* use analytics tools that track your individual usage patterns within the app.

2. Local Data Processing

One of the core features that ensures your privacy is our *local processing model*. When you use Labxio Free to analyze lab results, all computational analysis is performed *entirely on your device*. This means:

  • *No data is ever sent to any of our servers or external services* for processing.
  • This design ensures *complete data confidentiality*. Your health information never leaves your device.
  • Some features, such as optical character recognition (OCR) and lab report scanning, may require an internet connection to load certain open-source client-side libraries via a Content Delivery Networks (CDNs). However, all data analysis is still performed locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted during this process.
  • *Photo/Image Processing:* When you take pictures or upload photos of lab reports, these images are processed temporarily on your device for text extraction only. *Images are not stored* - neither locally nor on any servers - and are discarded immediately after processing.

3. Data Storage & Deletion

Labxio Free continues to operate with a **local-only data model**:

  • Any lab results or health data you analyze are processed *entirely on your device*.
  • We do not upload, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.
  • Images used for OCR (scanning lab reports) are processed temporarily on your device and discarded immediately after use.

If you uninstall Labxio Free or clear its app data, all locally stored information (like settings or analysis history) will be permanently deleted. We do not keep backups.

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Labxio Free is supported by advertisements. To deliver these ads, we use **Google AdMob** and **Google Play Services**.

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): AdMob may collect non-personal information such as your device's *Advertising ID*, approximate location, and other non-identifying usage data. This information helps deliver relevant ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.
    • You can reset or limit the use of your Advertising ID through your device's system settings.
    • Ads shown in Labxio Free may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your device preferences.
  • Google Play Services: Required to support ad delivery and core app functionality.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): Used to load open-source client-side libraries for PDF rendering and optical character recognition (OCR). No personal or health data is transmitted during this process.

We do not share any personally identifying information with advertisers.

5. Children's Privacy

Labxio Free is intended for users who are *18 years of age and older*.

  • The app may display ads from Google AdMob. These ads are not specifically directed toward children.
  • We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13.
  • Parents and guardians are responsible for monitoring the use of Labxio Free by minors.

6. International Privacy

Because Labxio Free does not collect or store personal data, there are no cross-border data transfers from our side.

  • For users in the **European Union (EU)** and other regions, **Google AdMob** manages compliance with applicable privacy regulations (such as GDPR).
  • Users can manage ad personalization directly through their device settings (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization").
